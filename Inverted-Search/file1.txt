The library is a quiet place where people come to read and learn. Students sit at long tables, focusing on books and notes,
 while others search for information on different topics. The peaceful environment helps everyone concentrate better and 
 improve their understanding. Outside the library, the garden is full of green plants and colorful flowers. Birds can be heard 
 singing, and a gentle breeze moves through the trees. Many people enjoy sitting there to relax, think, or simply enjoy nature.
In the evening, lights turn on inside the building, and a few visitors continue reading. The calm and silent atmosphere remains,
 making it an ideal place for studying even at night.
