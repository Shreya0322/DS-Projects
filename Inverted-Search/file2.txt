The city park is a peaceful place where people come to relax and enjoy their time. In the morning, some visitors prefer 
reading books on benches, while others walk along the paths and observe the natural environment. The fresh air and open space 
help everyone feel calm and refreshed. During the evening, the park becomes more lively as families and friends gather to spend 
time together. Children play games, and many people sit quietly, enjoying the beauty of the surroundings. The soft lights and gentle 
breeze create a comfortable atmosphere for relaxation. At night, the park slowly becomes silent, and only a few people remain. 
The quiet place offers a sense of peace, allowing visitors to think and reflect. Just like the library, it remains a special place 
for both learning and relaxation.
